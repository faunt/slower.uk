Yearly Privacy Policy

Yearly is designed to keep your data with you.

What Yearly stores

Yearly stores the information you create in the app, including selected date ranges, labels, and color settings.

Where data is stored

Your data is stored locally on your device.

If iCloud is available and enabled for Yearly, the app may also sync that data through Apple services, including CloudKit and iCloud key-value storage, so your information can stay in sync across your devices.

What Slower does not do

Yearly does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Slower does not run custom backend servers for storing your app data.

Third-party services

When iCloud sync is enabled, Apple processes data as part of providing its iCloud services. That processing is governed by Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about Yearly, contact david@slower.uk.
