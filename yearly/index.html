See the whole year before it slips into fragments.

Yearly is built for the kind of planning that does not fit neatly inside a single week. School breaks, custody schedules, annual leave, seasonal work, renovation windows, training blocks, shared calendars, and the long stretches of life that need room to breathe all make more sense when you can see the whole year.
